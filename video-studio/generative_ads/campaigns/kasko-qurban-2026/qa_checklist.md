# KASKO Qurban 2026 QA Checklist

Use this before any MP4 is sent to media buying or stakeholders.

## Brand

- Xalq Sigorta logo is exact and not AI-distorted.
- AZPETROL identity remains green and recognizable.
- Red/green palette feels festive but controlled.
- Visual tone is premium insurance advertising, not generic retail sale.

## Offer

- Main message is understandable without audio.
- Dates read exactly: `25 may - 5 iyun`.
- Gift mechanic reads as an Azpetrol fuel card.
- `750 AZN+` appears only as overlay or approved graphic.

## Legal / Eligibility

- Terms do not contradict the approved campaign brief.
- Exclusions are not over-compressed into misleading claims.
- No unapproved prize amount or card value is invented by the model.

## Motion

- First second is thumb-stopping and readable.
- Camera motion is smooth, not shaky.
- Final 0.8 seconds are stable enough for CTA.
- No major object drift between frames.

## AI Artifacts

- No malformed Azerbaijani letters in foreground.
- No fake UI elements or random icons.
- No warped car wheels, fuel hoses, cards, or gift box edges.
- No watermarks or visible model artifacts.

## Export

- 9:16 vertical.
- 10 seconds.
- Works without audio.
- CTA and headline are inside Meta safe area.
