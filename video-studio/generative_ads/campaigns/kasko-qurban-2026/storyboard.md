# KASKO Qurban 2026 Storyboard

Format: Meta Reels, 9:16, 10 seconds, sound-optional.

## Creative Principle

The ad should feel like a premium sponsored social card that comes alive. The
approved poster supplies the campaign world: Azpetrol pump, red car, gift box,
fuel cards, green/red holiday energy. The model should create motion and depth;
final copy stays locked in overlay.

## Timeline

| Time | Beat | Visual | Motion | Locked overlay |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0-1.5s | Open Graph hook | Sponsored card over soft campaign backdrop | Card float, subtle push-in, soft green/red sweep | Sponsored, Xalq Sigorta, campaign headline, CTA hint |
| 1.5-4.0s | Offer reveal | Card opens into fuel pump, red car, gift cards | 3D parallax, light sweep, ribbon shimmer | KASKO al, yanacaq kartı qazan |
| 4.0-7.5s | Simple terms | Clean terms cards over branded motion plate | Staggered slide-in, stable text zone | 25 MAY - 5 İYUN, 750 AZN+, AZPETROL |
| 7.5-10.0s | CTA settle | Final brand card and approved visual | Slow settle, last 0.8s still enough for reading | Avtomobilinizi qoruyun, yanacaq kartı qazanın |
