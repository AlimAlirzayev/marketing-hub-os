# Xalq Sigorta - Georgia Travel Insurance Reel

## Creative Direction

Format: 9:16 Instagram Reel, 15 seconds.

Tone: natural Azerbaijani blogger / creator speaking to followers. The video should feel like a useful warning from a real person, not like an official insurance announcement.

Core emotion: "You are excited about the trip, but do not let one forgotten document create stress at the border."

Brand role: Xalq Sigorta appears as the easy solution at the end, not as the main speaker from the beginning.

## Generation Prompt

Create a vertical 9:16 Instagram Reel style UGC ad in Azerbaijani for Xalq Sigorta travel insurance. Make it feel like a real Azerbaijani travel blogger warning followers before a Georgia trip, not a corporate commercial.

Scene 1: young Azerbaijani travel creator in Baku with a small suitcase and phone, excited about going to Georgia by train.

Scene 2: quick jump cuts of travel prep: train ticket on phone, suitcase zipper, passport, casual map route from Baku to Tbilisi, travel bag on a chair.

Scene 3: the creator looks into the camera with a warm but serious warning, as if telling followers "do not forget this".

Voice-over in natural Azerbaijani, casual blogger tone:
"Bir deqiqe, Gurcustana gedenler bunu esitsin. Bilet, otel, camadan hamisi yaxsidir, amma seyahat sigortasini unutmayin. Gurcustana giris ucun bu artiq lazimdir. Sonra serhedde 'kim dedi, harda yazilib?' elemeyin. Xalq Sigortadan onlayn alin, isiniz rahat olsun."

Use short on-screen Azerbaijani captions:
"Gurcustana gedirsiniz?"
"Sigortani unutmayin"
"Bu artiq lazimdir"
"Onlayn hell edin"
"Xalq Sigorta - Biz variq!"
"travel.xalqsigorta.az"

Visual style: authentic phone-shot reel, handheld, quick jump cuts, daylight, travel-prep energy, clean red black white brand accents, subtle motion, real human facial emotion, no official newsreader tone, no competitor logos, no PASHA branding.

End with a clean red/black/white brand card: Xalq Sigorta, Biz variq!, travel.xalqsigorta.az.
