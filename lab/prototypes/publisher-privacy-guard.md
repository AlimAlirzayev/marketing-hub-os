# Publisher Privacy Guard

**Status:** do-now

**Score:** 10/10

**Topic:** Publisher privacy guard for child and family imagery

**Goal:** Prevent unsafe use of child, family, event, or UGC imagery in public marketing.

**System integration idea:** Publisher and Media Studio flag minors/family imagery, ask for consent proof, and prefer synthetic or non-identifiable alternatives.

**Acceptance:**
- Minor/family imagery forces human review before publishing.
- Checklist asks for consent, audience scope, necessity, and safer substitutions.
- External AI editing/generation with minor imagery is blocked unless explicitly approved.

**Dependencies:**
- publisher
- atelier
- mediaforge
- config/agent_permissions.json

**Risks:**
- AI manipulation harm
- consent gap
- public reposting risk
- brand safety failure

**Next action:** Add this checklist to the next Publisher or Media Studio safety pass.

**Evidence:**
- [📸 Parents Warned Not to Post Children's Photos Online](https://t.me/perplexity/1034)
- [official source](https://www.nationalcrimeagency.gov.uk/news/new-guidance-for-parents-and-carers-as-ai-manipulated-images-of-children-become-a-growing-concern)
