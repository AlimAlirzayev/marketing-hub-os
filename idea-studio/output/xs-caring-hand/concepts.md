## The 5 concepts

### C1 — "Əl" anthology *(caring-hand-miniature · household-object-as-metaphor)*

45–60s hero film + 4×15s cutdowns. A realistic miniature Azerbaijani
world (film-set craft, macro textures, tilt-shift) hit by small
disasters; a giant photoreal hand descends each time holding one
ordinary **red** household object that becomes the instrument of care:

1. Flooded courtyard (tiny cars, raincoats) → red **sponge** absorbs the
   flood *(əmlak)*
2. Model shop on fire → red-handled **cloche** lowered, flames snuffed
   *(yanğın/biznes)*
3. Hail over a miniature parking lot → red **umbrella** opens over the
   cars *(KASKO)*
4. Warm beat, no hand: family at a tiny evening window, lights on —
   the world at peace *(rahatlıq — what the care was for)*

Endline (financial-restraint-az): **"Hər dünyanın üstündə bir əl var."**
Sub: "Xalq Sigorta. Sakit yaşamaq üçün." Contact lockup per brand kit.
The recurring red object = distinctive brand asset (evidence.md rule).

### C2 — "Sığorta darıxdırıcıdır." *(bernbach-ddb · self-indicting headline + proof-rich reversal)*

Print/feed series, charcoal type on ink, red divider. Headlines admit
the category's flaw, the reversal lands the truth:
- "Sığorta darıxdırıcıdır. Ödəniş gününə qədər."
- "İllərlə heç nə olmur. Bir gün hər şey olur."
- "Ən yaxşı halda bizi heç vaxt görməyəcəksiniz."
Body: one checkable fact each (183, onlayn dəqiqələr içində, real
claims mechanics). Runs as the anthology's print/feed companion.

### C3 — "Adi cəsarət" anthem *(wieden-kennedy · democratized hero)*

60s anthem: ordinary courage in insider detail (a shop's first 6am
opening; the first drive home with a newborn; keys to a first car),
quiet VO building to a 3-word landing: "Cəsarətin sığortası var."
Risk acknowledged: this is the parody-attracting corporate-poetry
formula (W+K's own anti-pattern) unless the insider detail is real.

### C4 — "Xalq Xəbərdarlığı" brand act *(droga-provocation · product-as-proof)*

Not an ad: a real, free severe-weather/dolu SMS+push warning service for
EVERYONE (customer or not), branded and launched as an act of care; the
caring-hand film announces it. The mechanic IS the message.

### C5 — "Balaca Bakı" lore channel *(internet-native-meme · brand-as-character + lore threads)*

A persistent miniature-Baku diorama world as an ongoing TikTok/IG
series: recurring tiny characters, the hand as a recurring presence,
comment-driven storylines (audience suggests what the hand should fix
next week), seasonal arcs. A channel strategy, not a campaign.

## Winning creative brief — C1 "Əl" (for /post + mediaforge + audio)

**The idea (Post-it test):** Böyük bir əl, balaca bir dünyanın
fəlakətini adi bir əşya ilə sakitcə düzəldir.
**One truth:** fəlakət anında sığorta = yuxarıdan uzanan əl.
**Job/metric:** brand (fame/feeling) — shares, saves, comment sentiment,
brand-lift; explicitly NOT CTR.

### Film craft (harmony.md applied)

- **Arousal curve:** hook high (crisis mid-action, 0–2s cold open, no
  logo) → modulation per vignette → pre-climax breath (scene 4 stillness)
  → calm brand close.
- **3-beat color script:** crisis = cool desaturated charcoal/ink world →
  hand enters = neutral, red object the only saturation → relief = warmth
  rises, brand red owns the final frame (endline + lockup).
- **Beat-anchored impacts:** the sponge TOUCH, cloche SEAL, umbrella
  SNAP-open land ON beats; skip beats between vignettes to breathe.
- **Figure-ground lock:** one dominant subject per frame; clean
  backgrounds; readable at thumbnail in 300ms.
- **Sound (audio-studio brief):** foley-first — squelch (sponge), hiss
  dying under the cloche, patter-then-silence (umbrella), distant
  laughter + fire crackle (scene 4). Sparse piano only from scene 3;
  no VO until the endline. One contrapuntal option: near-silence at the
  cloche seal (the loudest visual, the quietest sound).
- **Scene grammar per caring-hand DNA:** establish crisis 2–3s → hand
  enters with ONE object 2s → macro-texture job 3–4s → relief 2s.

### Per-studio direction

- **mediaforge:** style anchors — "realistic film-set miniature diorama,
  tilt-shift, macro texture detail, giant photoreal human hand from
  above, soft daylight, charcoal-ink palette with single red accent
  object"; NEVER cartoon/toy-plastic look (brand kit). Model resolver
  picks the dialect that holds hand realism; hand quality is the KILL
  criterion in render review.
- **social-studio:** stills = frame-grabs of the 3 metaphors + scene 4
  for feed (1080×1350), footer per brand kit; style `editorial-documentary`.
- **copy-studio:** voice `financial-restraint-az`. Endline "Hər dünyanın
  üstündə bir əl var." — no exclamation, brand arrives last. C2
  headlines run as the print companion wave 2.
- **/publish:** hero on IG Reels/TikTok; cutdowns per product line;
  stills as carousel.

### Anti-patterns on watch (from the DNAs)

Two objects in one scene · explaining the metaphor in VO/caption · the
hand as menace/prank · over-clean CG (must feel touchable) · logo on
the hand · running the flood scene during a real flood (pull rule) ·
metronome beat-cutting.

### What would make it fail

If the hand doesn't render photoreal, the film reads as cheap CG and the
craft-flex signal inverts — kill at render review, fall back to C2
(print series) which is shippable today.
