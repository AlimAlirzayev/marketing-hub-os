# Style anchor — Financial-services editorial

Reusable mood block for any Xalq Sigorta or sibling-brand campaign.
Drop into Layer 9 of a master_template prompt.

## Reference campaigns (real, look them up)

- **Mont Blanc** — 2024 travel/luggage campaigns. Restrained, no neon,
  rich shadows, hero metal/glass props.
- **Hermès "Petit h"** — documentary series. Hands at work, calm
  lighting, editorial restraint.
- **Apple "Shot on iPhone" travel category** — candid framing, real
  light, no glossy retouch.
- **Magnum Photos travel/editorial** — documentary realism baseline.

## Reference photographers (cite for tone)

- Annie Leibovitz (corporate portrait, controlled lighting)
- Rinko Kawauchi (soft, intimate, atmospheric)
- Steve McCurry (Caucasus + Central Asia editorial)

## Lighting baseline

- Late morning or golden afternoon, NEVER harsh midday.
- 4500–5200K color temperature.
- 1:3 to 1:5 key-to-fill ratio (not flat, not dramatic).
- Window light or soft north light preferred.

## Composition baseline

- Rule of thirds, subject at 2/3 vertical.
- Lead lines from window/road/track guide the eye.
- Negative space deliberate, never accidental.

## What this style ANCHOR is NOT

- ✗ Lifestyle stock photo (smiling-at-camera, evenly lit, no shadows)
- ✗ Luxury-watch advert (over-polished glamour)
- ✗ Sports brand energy (motion blur, saturation)
- ✗ Tech-product render (CGI cleanliness, studio softboxes)
