# Style DNA — `soft-maximalist`

Slow luxury. The frame is full but breathes. Texture-rich, painterly,
Vermeer-with-a-cellphone. The viewer wants to touch the image.

## Lineage

- **Mariella Roser** for Hermès — textured layering, hand-prominent framing.
- **Erik Madigan Heck** Vogue editorial — color as composition.
- **Bottega Veneta 2023–2025** campaigns — muted oxblood / sage palettes.
- **Loewe** crafted-leather campaigns — material rendering as the subject.
- **Lyle Owerko** quiet luxury portraits.

Avoid: minimalism, geometric flat design, "clean lifestyle" stock.

## Subject treatment

- **Pose:** Subject often partially obscured — by a foreground textile,
  a fold of curtain, a vase, a piece of fruit. The frame is layered.
- **Hands prominent:** Holding, touching, gesturing, mid-craft. Hands are
  characters in the frame, not background.
- **Gaze:** Eyes closed OR soft middle-distance. Inward. Contemplative.
- **Number:** Solo strongly preferred. Pairs allowed if both are within
  reach of each other's hands.
- **Scale:** Tight medium — sometimes only the hands and a slice of face.

## Composition

- **Asymmetric.** Visual weight gathered on one side, breathing room on
  the other. Never balanced symmetry.
- **Three or four depth layers:** foreground texture, midground subject,
  background tone-on-tone. Each layer carries its own visual story.
- **Frame elements:** Curtain, doorway, plant, table edge, fabric drape —
  always something breaking the frame.
- **Crop:** Tight enough to feel intimate. Often the subject's full body
  is not shown.

## Light

- **Soft diffused window light** or overcast outdoors.
- **Painterly direction:** Vermeer-style — light enters from one side,
  drifts across the subject and falls into shadow on the far side.
- **Color temperature:** 3800-4500K, warmer than editorial-documentary.
  Slight golden cast accepted.
- **Shadows:** Long, soft, full of detail. Highlights never blown out.
- **No catchlight obsession** — the eye is often in shadow.

## Color story

- **Dominant palette:** Sage, oxblood, ivory, dusty rose, mustard, cream,
  unbleached linen, terracotta. Always muted.
- **Saturation register:** Low overall, but **variety** is high — many
  hues, each desaturated.
- **Contrast:** Mid. Never high-key bright, never deep-shadow drama.
- **Brand red discipline:** A muted version of `#E31E24` (toward
  burgundy `#8B2D31`) so it harmonizes with the palette. Pure brand red
  would scream in this voice.

## Material rendering

- **Tactile maximalism.** Linen weave, ceramic glaze, velvet pile,
  fingerprint-on-glass attention to surface.
- **Imperfection allowed.** Crumb on the table. Hair out of place. A
  chipped ceramic edge. The frame feels lived-in.
- **Real materials only.** Plastic discouraged; if present, vintage
  plastic with patina.

## Mood vocabulary

- **Pace:** Very slow. Suspended. Like reading a long book in the afternoon.
- **Intimacy distance:** Close. The viewer is *with* the subject, not
  watching from across the room.
- **Emotional register:** Reflective, content, private.
- **Story register:** A single private moment with no clear before or after.

## Lens & camera

- **Focal length:** 50mm or 85mm equivalent.
- **Aperture:** f/2.0 to f/2.8 — shallow, painterly.
- **ISO:** 400, slightly more grain than editorial.
- **Camera angle:** Often slightly below eye-level, looking up subtly.

## Style-specific exclusions

```
✗ Symmetric or centered compositions
✗ Clean / minimalist / geometric backgrounds
✗ High-key bright lighting or flat HDR
✗ Cool white or pure-white tones
✗ Subject perfectly visible — partial occlusion is part of the voice
✗ Pure-saturation brand red — shift to burgundy in this voice
✗ Plastic / glossy / new-out-of-box materials
✗ Three or more subjects
✗ Direct gaze at camera
```

## When to choose this style

Premium home products, gift-giving moments, lifestyle storytelling that
needs to feel hand-made not factory-finished. Xalq Sigorta property
insurance, family insurance, anything domestic.

**NOT for:** Travel, fast products, B2B, anything urgent.
