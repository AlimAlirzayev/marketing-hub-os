# v3 — master_template × editorial-documentary DNA

Built by `/post` slash command. Style DNA `editorial-documentary` overrides
Layers 2-3 + 9 + 11 of the master template. Brand defaults from brand_kit
carry Layers 1, 4-8.

```
=== HARD CONSTRAINTS ===
ONE photographic background. 4:5 vertical, 1080x1350.
Background plate only. NO headline text, NO logo, NO contact text, NO
fake readable writing — overlays applied in post.
Subject count: exactly one couple in frame.
Scene: interior of a modern intercity passenger train, Baku-Tbilisi route.

=== CAMERA AND LENS (editorial-documentary DNA) ===
50mm full-frame equivalent. f/2.8. ISO 200 with organic film grain.
Medium shot, waist-up framing. Slight low angle (~chair-armrest height).
Subjects right of center, window left.

=== LIGHTING (editorial-documentary DNA) ===
Single key: soft window light from camera right. Late morning warmth, 4800K.
Window glass diffuses the source. 1:4 fill ratio. Retained shadow modeling
on subjects' off-side cheek and jaw. Small soft catchlights in both eyes,
single point each. NO artificial spot lights, NO HDR look, NO backlit halo.

=== SUBJECT IDENTITY ===
Caucasus-Azerbaijani features, ages 28-34. Confident, relaxed, mid-action
captured between two beats — NOT posed. Lips closed or faintly parted,
quiet contentment. NEVER a teeth-out smile.
Woman: shoulder-length dark brown hair, minimal natural makeup, small
gold stud earrings. Cream knit sweater, NO patterns, NO logos.
Man: short well-groomed beard, dark hair, plain charcoal henley.
Both NOT looking at the camera. Their gaze tracks the railway viaduct
view through the window. Relaxed posture, leaning slightly toward the
view. Seasoned travelers, not first-timers.

=== BRAND PROPS ===
Red hard-shell suitcase: LOWER-LEFT foreground, 30% edge-cropped.
Color HEX #E31E24 (Xalq Sigorta brand red). Matte modern, no scuffs,
no visible brand name. Reading-side handle stowed.
Smartphone: in woman's hand, screen toward camera. Display shows
abstract red shield silhouette + 3 horizontal data bars on white.
ZERO readable text, NO letters, NO numbers, NO logo.
Passports: in man's hand, navy blue (Azerbaijan style), slight leather
wear, ONE folded paper ticket peeking out the top.

=== SCENE BACKGROUND ===
Outside window: a real Caucasus railway moment. Steel viaduct OR mountain
cutting, mid-distance. Hazy warm daylight (not postcard-saturated). Far
mountains with soft atmospheric perspective. No towns visible.
Train interior: modern Stadler/CAF aluminum trim, dark navy fabric seats,
soft tray-table edge in frame. Small unlit decorative reading lamp above
the woman.

=== BRAND ATMOSPHERE ===
Far-LEFT edge 20% width: feathered burgundy fade #5C0F12 → transparent.
Atmospheric haze, NOT a wash, NOT a band. Viewer should not consciously
notice it.
Window glass over the bridge view: transparent shield outline reflection,
~12% opacity, ~120px wide, soft edges. Second-glance discovery, not a
focal point. NO glow, NO fantasy effect.

=== NEGATIVE SPACE FOR OVERLAY ===
Upper-LEFT 480x540px: calm dark zone for Azerbaijani headline. The
deepest gradient lives here. No faces, props, or competing detail.
Bottom 180px: clean footer zone. No body parts, no suitcase handles,
no phone. Blurred floor / seat edge only.

=== STYLE ANCHOR (editorial-documentary DNA — verbatim) ===
- Editorial documentary photography, Magnum Photos travel category,
  Annie Leibovitz corporate portrait grammar.
- Subject mid-action, gaze off-frame toward the environment.
- Single soft key light from window, 4800K, 1:4 fill ratio, retained
  shadow modeling.
- Muted earth palette with one saturated brand-color accent prop.
- Visible skin texture, pores allowed, natural hand anatomy, fabric
  weave visible.
- Slow editorial pace. Quiet observed moment.

=== QUALITY DIRECTIVES ===
Visible skin texture - pores, faint freckles. Natural hand anatomy: all 10
fingers correct, knuckles defined. Fabric weave visible on close inspection.
Film grain ISO 200 equivalent - subtle, organic. No painterly smearing,
no AI plastic skin, no warped suitcase wheels.

=== EXCLUSION LIST (master ai-tells + editorial-documentary specific) ===
✗ Any visible text or readable signage (NO fake brand names, NO ad copy,
  NO numbers on phone, NO writing on suitcase, NO destination boards)
✗ Watermarks (Shutterstock, Getty, Pollinations)
✗ Plastic AI skin or smooth-airbrushed faces
✗ Subjects facing or looking at the camera
✗ Teeth-out smiles or any "lifestyle catalog" expressions
✗ Studio softbox-everywhere HDR-flat lighting
✗ Vibrant postcard saturation
✗ Brand red used as wash, band, or background fill
✗ Multiple couples or background passengers
✗ Three or more subjects in frame
✗ Motion blur on subjects
✗ Backlit halo / rim light glamour
✗ Stock-photo composition (centered subject, no foreground layering)
✗ Extra fingers, fused fingers, warped hand anatomy
✗ Distorted suitcase wheels
✗ Visible camera operator reflection in window
```
