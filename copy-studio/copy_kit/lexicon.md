# Xalq Sigorta lexicon — preferred & banned words

The brand's choice of words is half the voice. This file is the
arbitration table when the writer is debating two near-synonyms.

## Preferred (use these)

### Azerbaijani

| Use | Instead of |
|---|---|
| sığorta | sığortalanma (more verbose, less marketing-friendly) |
| məhsul | servis (məhsul is the brand's preferred framing) |

## Banned (do NOT use)

### Banned in any language

- "Crush it / game-changer / next-level / disrupt" — startup speak
- "Don't miss out" / "Hurry" / "Limited time" — desperation register
- "Unlock" / "Discover" / "Reveal" — clickbait verbs
- "We're excited to announce" / "We are thrilled" — corporate filler
- "Your dream vacation / Your family's safety" (in vague form) — generic
- "At Xalq Sigorta we believe…" — never lead with brand

### Banned in AZ specifically

- "Möhtəşəm" / "fenomenal" / "inanılmaz" — over-used hype adjectives
- "Sevimli müştərilərimiz" — paternalistic
- "Bilirikkı…" / "Biz hesab edirikkı…" — corporate filler
- "Hörmətli müştərilər" — formal but old-fashioned, use "siz" form instead
- "Səbrsizliklə gözləyirik" — desperate
- "Heç bir problem olmadan" — double-negative, unclear

### Banned in EN specifically

- "Best-in-class" — meaningless superlative
- "We've got you covered" — insurance industry cliché
- "Peace of mind" — most overused financial-services phrase
- "Travel smart / Stay safe!" — patronizing
- "Hassle-free experience" — adjective stacking

## Spelling and formatting

- **Brand name:** always "Xalq Sigorta" (two words, both capitalized).
  Never "XalqSigorta", never "Xalq Sığorta" (without dot on i).
- **Hotline:** "183" with no leading zero, no parentheses.
- **Website:** "xalqsigorta.az" lowercase, no www.
- **Dates:** "1 yanvar 2026" in AZ, "January 1, 2026" in EN.
- **Money:** "100 AZN" not "AZN 100", not "100 manat".
- **Numbers under 10:** spelled in copy ("üç gün"), digits in headlines/data.

## Word-count guardrails

| Asset | AZ words | EN words |
|---|---|---|
| Headline | 4-7 | 4-7 |
| Sub-headline | 6-12 | 5-10 |
| Body sentence | 12-18 | 10-15 |
| Instagram caption | 80-150 | 80-150 |
| LinkedIn caption | 120-200 | 120-180 |
| Reel script 15s | 25-35 | 25-30 |
| Reel script 30s | 50-65 | 45-55 |
| Email subject | 6-9 | 5-8 |

Going over → cut. Voice's power is restraint.
