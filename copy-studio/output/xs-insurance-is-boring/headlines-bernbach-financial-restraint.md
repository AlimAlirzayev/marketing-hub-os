## Why this works (device notes)

- The admission ("darıxdırıcıdır", "heç nə olmur", "bizi görməyəcəksiniz")
  buys believability for the reversal — Bernbach's candor mechanic.
- Each reversal carries ONE checkable fact (183 / onlayn dəqiqələr /
  claims readiness) — wit that smuggles a fact.
- Category convention broken on purpose: AZ finance never admits a flaw.
- No exclamation marks; urgency lives in the words (voice rule).
