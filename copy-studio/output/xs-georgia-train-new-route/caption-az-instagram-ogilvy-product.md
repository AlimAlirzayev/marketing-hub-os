## Why this works in this voice

- Headline = "the forgotten thing" Ogilvy pattern (cf. "How to Avoid Holes
  in Your Socks"). Frames the reader's problem, then names the brand's
  solution.
- Sub = the regulatory fact, locked.
- Body opens with the customer's situation (the train schedule, the
  short trip), THEN brings in the brand fact (3 minutes online). Ogilvy
  signature: situation before product.
- "Səyahət iki gün çəkmir; sənədlərin də hazırlanması iki günə
  qalmasın" — wry parallel construction, Ogilvy dry wit.
- CTA = two channels listed, no urgency.
- Period as primary punctuation. Semicolon used once for the parallel.
