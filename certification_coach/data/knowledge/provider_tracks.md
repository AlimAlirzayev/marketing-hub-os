# Provider And Track Notes

## Google Skillshop

Google Ads and Google Analytics badges are strong early proof for measurement,
paid search, tagging, funnels, and reporting discipline. They are most valuable
when paired with a campaign structure or measurement brief.

## Meta Blueprint

Meta Certified Media Buying Professional is a stronger senior signal than basic
social media certificates. Treat it as a capstone: the learner should already
understand campaign objectives, signals, optimization, creative testing,
delivery, troubleshooting, and measurement.

## LinkedIn Marketing Academy

LinkedIn Advertising Fundamentals is useful for B2B targeting and Campaign
Manager fluency. LinkedIn Marketing Strategy is more strategic: use it for
full-funnel planning, organic plus paid alignment, audience proof, and executive
campaign thinking.

## TikTok Academy

TikTok Media Buying is creative-led and proctored. It should be attempted only
after hands-on practice with hooks, creative diagnostics, event quality,
optimization, and platform policy basics.

## HubSpot Academy

HubSpot certifications are good momentum builders for content, inbound, and
social operating rhythms. They are not enough alone for senior proof; pair them
with a calendar, channel plan, or content measurement system.

## Semrush Academy

Semrush SEO learning is strongest when it becomes a real SEO deliverable:
keyword research, SERP intent, competitor gap analysis, technical checks, and an
article or brief produced through the SEO Studio.

## CXL

CXL Growth Marketing is a premium, time-heavy path for experimentation and
full-funnel growth maturity. It should be approved deliberately because it is
paid and has a larger time commitment.
