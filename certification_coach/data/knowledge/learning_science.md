# Certification Learning Science

This module uses a mastery path, not a passive reading path. The learner should
move through retrieval, spacing, feedback, repair, and proof before official
exam handoff.

## Retrieval Practice

Practice questions are useful because they force recall and reveal gaps. The
coach uses original mock questions only. A mock is not a shortcut to live exam
content; it is a diagnostic loop with rationales and weak-topic repair.

## Spacing And Interleaving

Readiness improves when study is distributed across sessions and topic types are
mixed. The journey engine therefore counts study blocks and scenario drills
separately, then requires mock attempts after practice instead of immediately
after a single reading pass.

Sources:

- https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1111/j.1467-9280.2006.01693.x
- https://psycnet.apa.org/record/2006-06479-001

## Feedback And Weakness Repair

Feedback matters only when it changes the next action. After a low mock score or
missed questions, the learner must complete weak-topic repair before the coach
opens the human approval handoff.

Source:

- https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.3102/003465430298487

## Mastery Gate

The official exam handoff is blocked until the learner has current official
source verification, a baseline, study blocks, scenario drills, strong mock
attempts, weak-topic repair, and portfolio proof. This protects assessment
integrity while making the learning path much more likely to produce real skill.
